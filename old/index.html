ABOUT US

“The point of training is to increase speed at which one can be precise.” – Cesar Luis Menotti, 1978 World Cup winning coach of Argentina.

Established in 2014, Football World is one of the leading football academies for football coaching in Thane. Specialists in grassroots training, we train children from the age of 4 years onwards till youth. Our training methods includes holistic development of children.

Our method of training develops players technically and also encourages them to make their own decisions. The importance of making their own decisions leads to development of more creative players. In addition, the speed to take these decisions and execution of the decisions is focussed heavily across all training sessions. Players are put through fun and physically demanding drills with development and growth being the only outcome of the session. We ensure safety of all our students in our sessions.

Inculcating our core values of Passion, Dedication, Commitment and Respect, we strive to develop not only better players but also better human beings.

Developing players in the game and in life.

Integrating values and attitude to make a player an ambassador of the game forever.

TESTIMONIAL


THE TEAM

Football World was started by three individuals, who are also the head coaches of the academy. They are responsible for the development and execution of the training models across all centres. With the increasing speed and complexity of the game, the head coaches ensure that the training methods across all the centres are up to date with the global coaching models.

Varun D'souza

Co-founder & Head Coach

Varun D'souza

He is an AFC C License Coach. A stickler for discipline, he makes sure players are always putting their 100% and never gives up until the final whistle. He is always pushing the players to their limit and demanding more effort from them, which is a core aspect of the game. He is also an AIFF Certified Grassroots Leader.

Krystalle Aguiar

Co-founder & Head Coach

Krystalle Aguiar

She is an AIFF D License Coach. She works passionately with the youngest age group starting from 4 years onward. She makes sure the children are solid in their basics, making it easier for them to develop faster as the children grow older. She is also a Premier Skills Certified Coach.

Maclin D'penha

Co-founder & Head Coach

Maclin D'Penha

He is an AFC B License Coach. A tactically brilliant and creative coach, he ensures that players think during a session/match to create more advantageous situations for themselves and the team. He is also a Mumbai City FC Certified Grassroots coach.
